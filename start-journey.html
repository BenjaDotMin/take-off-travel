Time to start your journey

Take Off in Travel is here to introduce you to one of the biggest – and most exciting – industries in the world

When you think of big business and opportunities to build a long career, you might not immediately think of travel and tourism.

But what if we told you one in 10 jobs in the UK prior to the pandemic were connected to the industry? Now you start to get an idea of how big the industry is and see what a great place it is to find your perfect job.

The outbound travel sector – companies taking people abroad for holidays or to do business – supports 720,000 jobs in the UK, according to research carried out for the travel association Abta. When you also take into account the sectors serving people coming into the country, and those travelling within it, that number of employees rises to a incredible 4.1 million, according to the World Travel & Tourism Council.

And when you think the industry is still recovering from a near-total shutdown during the Covid-19 pandemic and is predicted to grow beyond pre-pandemic levels in the coming years, those numbers are even more impressive.

A role in travel and tourism has lots of benefits. If you’re lucky, you may get to see different parts of the world and you’ll certainly get to meet lots of people from different walks of life. And as many people will tell you, once you get the travel bug, it’s an industry you won’t want to leave.

Careers in travel and tourism Take Off in Travel was created 10 years ago, as companies within the industry wanted young people and their parents to have a better understanding of what was on offer – that a job in travel wasn’t just something you did for a summer before getting a “proper career”.

Across this website, we’ll try to show off our amazing industry, and highlight some reasons why you might want to find out more about joining it.

Sometimes travel is about getting from A to B, but at its best it’s about creating amazing memories and going on journeys you’ll never forget.

The same could be said for a career in travel. Explore this site to find out more.
